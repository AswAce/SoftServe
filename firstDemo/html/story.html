One piece arks:

The East Blue

Is the introductory saga of the series. Monkey D. Luffy meets Shanks as a young boy living in East Blue. This is where he recruits most of his crew and, ultimately, where the adventure begins.

Alabasta

The desert kingdom of Arabasta has a massive breaks out between the kingdom's royal guards and the rebels. Straw hats want to stop it However, the true perpetrator of the war, are in their way.

Sky Island

The Straw Hats arrive on the sky island Skypiea in search of gold, only to find out that the island is in danger of being destroyed by the cruel "god", Enel.

Enies Lobby

The Straw Hats arrive at Enies Lobby aboard Rocketman to rescue Nico Robin and Franky. Standing in their way, however, are the forces of the World Government, including its deadly assassin organization, CP9. A massive battle ensues between the two sides when Luffy declares war against the World Government.

Thriller bark

The Straw hats arrive at a haunted island/ship called Thriller Bark, in which their shadows are stolen by Warlord of the Sea Gecko Moria and must hurry to get them back before the sun rises. After defeating Moria, another Warlord shows up to make the Straw Hats' situation worse.

Sabaody Archipelago

This arc marks a turning point for the Straw Hat Pirates since they were all utterly defeated by powerful opponents such as Admiral Kizaru, the Pacifista, Bartholomew Kuma, and Sentomaru, even though they have grown stronger in their own right.

Amazon Island

After his defeat at the hands of Kuma in Sabaody Archipelago, Luffy is sent flying and crash lands on Amazon Lily, an island exclusive to women that executes males on sight.

Impel down

Luffy puts finding his crew on hold in favor of rescuing his brother Ace from the underwater prison Impel Down.

Marineford war

A great war erupt between Whitebeard pirates and Marines when they try to execity Ace. This is when Luffy shows.

Mermid island

The Straw Hat Pirates finally head for Fish-Man Island after a two-year hiatus. But many surprises await them as they head for their undersea destination and even more so once they reach it.

Punk hazard

An emergency call guide the crew to a strange island

Dressrossa

The Straw Hats and Trafalgar Law, having formed an alliance set sail for Dressrosa; There they aim to take down the nation's king, Warlord of the Sea Donquixote Doflamingo.

Zou

When they arrive to Zou, however, they discover that Sanji has been swept up in a set of personal and political entanglements, and that Zou has been subjected to a siege by Jack of the Beasts Pirates.

Wholecake

The group seeks to rescue Sanji from his arranged marriage. In the midst, they become involved in a plot seeking to take down Big Mom herself.

Wano

The Ninja-Pirate-Mink-Samurai Alliance gather in Wano Country and prepare for the battle against the Beasts Pirates.
